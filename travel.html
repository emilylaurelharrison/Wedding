Travel


Getting there

Fly

Charles de Gaulle is the closest airport.
From there we recommend hiring a car or booking a taxi (approx. 1.5 hour drive)
Alternatively, there is a train from Paris St. Lazare to Val de Reuil (approx. 30 mins drive).

Eurostar

You can take the Eurostar to Paris Gare du Nord.
From there we recommend hiring a car or booking a taxi (approx. 1.5 hour drive).
Alternatively, there is a train from Paris St. Lazare to Val de Reuil (approx. 30 min drive).

Ferry

There are multiple ferry options, all with slightly different crossing and drive times.
Newhaven > Dieppe (+1.5 hour drive)
Portsmouth > Le Havre (+1.5 hour drive)
Portsmouth > Caen (+2 hour drive)

If you are having any problems planning your journey, please let us know. We've done lots of research and can put you in touch with each other to share transfers!
