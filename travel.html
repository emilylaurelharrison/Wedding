Travel


Getting there

We recommend booking all travel as early as you can.
If you are having any problems planning your journey, please let us know!

Fly

Charles de Gaulle is the closest international airport to the chateau.
From there we recommend hiring a car (1.5 hour drive).
There is a train from Paris St. Lazare to Val de Reuil (approx. 30 mins from the chateau) but you would need to travel across Paris first.

Eurostar

Similar to above, if you take the Eurostar to Paris Gare du Nord, you can then hire a car or take the train to Val de Reuil.

Ferry

There are multiple ferry options, all with slightly different crossing and drive times.
Newhaven > Dieppe (+1.5 hour drive)
Portsmouth > Le Havre (+1.5 hour drive)
Portsmouth > Caen (+2 hour drive)


During the weekend

We will have a taxi service on hand for the weekend to shuttle between accommodation and the chateau.
We'll add the contact details for the taxi closer to the time.
